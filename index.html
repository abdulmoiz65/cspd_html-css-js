About CSPD

The Centre for Skills & Professional Development (CSPD) at Mohammad Ali Jinnah University equips students, young professionals, and industry experts with worldwide certifications, skills, and ongoing learning to thrive in a changing employment market.

CSPD was founded to bridge academics and industry in skills. It creates unique, career-relevant training programs using the university's academic strengths and industry relationships.

CSPD Vision

To be recognized as one of the leading centers for fostering industry-relevant skills, entrepreneurial mindset, and professional excellence, allowing individuals to contribute to a knowledge-driven society.

CSPD Mission

The Centre for Skills & Professional Development (CSPD) at Mohammad Ali Jinnah University offers inclusive, ethical, and high-quality training programs to equip different learners with skills, technology, and a forward-thinking mindset. This equips learners to overcome obstacles, succeed professionally, and contribute to a prosperous and inventive community.

Welcome to CSPD – MAJU. Our mission is to nurture the next generation of professionals equipped with industry-relevant skills, ethics, and leadership abilities to create impact in an ever-evolving economy.

Our trainers are distinguished experts from academia and industry who bring real-world experience and interactive learning methods to every session.

We provide access to modern classrooms, digital labs, online learning tools, and course materials to ensure hands-on and effective training experiences.

CSPD collaborates with leading industry partners and international organizations to bring the latest global trends and certifications to our learners.

Our dedicated team comprises educators, administrators, and industry specialists who work together to deliver impactful, learner-focused programs.
